ETL Developer Job Brief:

We are seeking an experienced ETL Developer to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong
background in data warehousing and ETL development using Informatica PowerCenter. In this role, you
will be responsible for working with business and technical teams to understand data requirements,
designing and developing high-performance ETL solutions to load data into our data warehouse, and
supporting the ongoing maintenance and improvement of our ETL processes.

The successful candidate will be a self-starter with a passion for data and for delivering quality
solutions that meet the needs of our business.

ETL Developer Job Responsibilities :

Mentioned below are the major roles and responsibilities of an ETL Developer:

Designing and developing ETL solutions

Identifying data sources and setting up extraction processes

Transforming and cleaning data according to the business needs

Loading data into the data warehouse

Updating existing ETL jobs as per changing requirements

Monitoring ETL jobs and troubleshooting issues

Optimizing ETL processes for improved performance

Working closely with other teams such as Data Warehouse, BI, and Development team
Provide maintenance and support for the existing ETL jobs

ETL Developer Job Requirements :

The most crucial requirements of an ETL Developer are as follows:

Bachelor's degree in information technology, computer science, or related field;
3+ years of experience in ETL development and data warehousing;

Strong experience with SQL and relational databases (e.g., Oracle, Teradata, DB2);
Experience with ETL tools such as Informatica, DataStage, or SSIS;

Experience with Big Data platforms such as Hadoop, Hive, or Spark;

Excellent problem solving and troubleshooting skills;

Excellent project management abilities;

Capability to assess a firm's big-picture data requirements;

Ability to communicate clearly and effectively.

An experienced ETL Developer will have a strong understanding of data modeling, data cleansing and
data transformation techniques. They will be able to design and develop ETL processes to load data
into a target database while maintaining the integrity of the data. In addition, they will be responsible
for developing and supporting documentation for all ETL processes.

Moreover, the most successful ETL Developers will possess strong technical skills, as well as excellent
communication and interpersonal skills. They are detail-oriented and have the ability to think critically
in order to solve complex problems.
